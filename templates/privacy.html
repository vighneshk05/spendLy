Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — basic information about how you interact with the app, such as pages visited and actions taken.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to provide and improve the Spendly service:

  • To display your expense history and spending summaries.
  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • To diagnose bugs and improve app performance.

We will never sell your personal data or use it for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. Upon deletion, your data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for hosting and infrastructure. These providers are contractually obligated to handle your data securely and only as directed by us.

We do not share your personal data with any third party for their own marketing or business purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact information provided on our website. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
